ABOUT MIZUHARU

Mizuharu was founded in 2078 by Jhonrhey D., Francis D. and Frontly Hebwel P. , the reason behind is to help places that hardly has access to crystalline and safe drinking water. With our motto "Providing high quality water service purer than your soul". It might sound like mocking the reader but we really are mocking them (just kidding). In the beginning, Mizuharu was a small company focused on solving the global water problem. We worked hard on new ideas to make sure everyone had access to clean and safe water. One of our first big achievements was creating a water purifier that could make even the dirtiest water safe to drink. This success helped us grow and reach more people. As we expanded, we began building large projects to provide water to areas in need. We partnered with governments and organizations to set up desalination plants, water distribution systems, and wastewater treatment facilities. In the middle of the 22nd century, we introduced smart water management systems. These systems use advanced technology to monitor and improve how water is used, cutting down on waste and making sure water resources are managed well. Our solutions have become standard practice, helping communities and cities use water more efficiently. Today, Mizuharu is known worldwide for our dedication to quality, innovation, and sustainability. We continue to invest in new ideas to make sure everyone has access to clean water. The vision of our founders to solve water problems is still at the heart of everything we do. We believe that everyone has the right to clean water, and we are committed to making that a reality.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide accurate, transparent, and reliable water billing solutions to communities and businesses, ensuring efficient water usage management. We strive to empower customers with clear billing information and exceptional customer service, promoting responsible water consumption and sustainability for a better future.

To become the leading provider of innovative water billing systems that foster smarter, more sustainable water usage. We envision a future where our technology not only simplifies billing processes but also helps conserve water, making it accessible and affordable for all while contributing to the global effort of preserving our planet’s most precious resource.
